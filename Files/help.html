SpeakEasy Help

Sometimes there are bugs that prevent the app from connecting to TeamSpeak and sometimes you can get back to a working state by closing the main window and restarting the app.

In either case, please send me a pm on Overwolf's forums with any details about the bug you are experiencing and I will try my best to find a fix for you. My username is adamk33n3r.

Chat: Use this to chat with your friends with text.

Channels: List of all channels on the server. Click on to switch to it!

Toggles: Buttons that let you toggle your mic and speakers.

Client List: List of clients in channels that you are subscribed to. You can open a chat with users from here

Settings: Sliders in order to move both the talkers and notification alerts.
